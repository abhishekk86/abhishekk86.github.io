How I Work

Principles I keep coming back to.

Make the system understandable. Architecture should help teams move, not freeze them.

Design for production reality. Logs, metrics, failures, retries, upgrades, and support matter.

Prefer useful modernization. Better platforms come from staged progress, not big-bang rewrites.

Keep delivery close to design. A good architecture is one the team can actually build and operate.
